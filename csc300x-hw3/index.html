UNCG EATS

Are you a culinary adventurer in search of the perfect meal on campus? Look no further! We've curated a list of the most exceptional dishes that are sure to tantalize your taste buds. From fresh pizzas to mouthwatering sushi, this list has got you covered. So, put on your foodie hat and let's dive into the delightful world of flavors.

Slices by Tony.

Slices by Tony's is an easygoing pizza joint serving classic & creative slices, pies & Italian-American staples & specials.

Location: 401 Tate St, Greensboro, NC 27403

Hours: 10:30 AM–10 PM

Recommended Foods: Steak Special Sub, Baked Ziti Pizza, Chicken Al Pesto Pizza

Don's.

Simple, booth-lined Japanese restaurant specializing in hot entrees like donburi, teriyaki & ramen.

Location: 423 Tate St, Greensboro, NC 27403

Hours: 11 AM–3:30 PM, 5–10 PM

Recommended Foods: Gyoza, Chicken Katsu Rice Bowl, Chicken Udon Soup

East Coast Wings + Grill

American grill chain specializing in chicken wings in many flavors & levels of heat.

Location: 338 Tate St, Greensboro, NC 27403

Hours: 11 AM–9 PM

Recommended Foods: Philly Nachos, Big Dill Burger, Steak Burger
